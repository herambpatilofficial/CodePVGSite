About CodePVG

CodePVG is a community of highly motivated folks, interested in technology, startups, research and a range of different technological domains. We are the community who aspire to become the finest collegiate community, and we strive to build great things, conserve inter-disciplinary inclusive approach and escaping mediocrity!

Our Story

CodePVG is actually way more than a college society, we started it to build a college level community of students in PVG's College of Engineering, and it got officially recognized by college after 4 months of its operation including technological discussions, podcasts, and a lot of other things, currently, we're working to make this story even more interesting, and primarily working in following domains currently.
